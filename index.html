About

Journey To Web Development.

  • Humble Beginnings

    I remember when my dad brought home our first computer and setting it up. This was in the early 90s before most homes had internet. Our desktop was only used for games and typing. A few years later we bought a Gateway which had dial up internet, and my world changed. From AOL instant messenger, Xanga, MySpace to downloading music, computers were something I used daily. I would only use them on a surface level, and not understand what is going on behind the scenes.This surface level intrest stuck around until my brother a computer analyst told me to check out some courses on Front-End Web Development

  • May 2017 - March 2018

    Self Taught "Coder" to Full Stack Program

    I started using online resources to teach myself HTML and CSS in the begiining of May 2017. Using resources such as Lynda.com, freecodecamp.com. After creating my first "Hello World, I was hooked! A few months of learning on my own, I continued to hear about coding programs. I decided to apply for General Assembly's Full Stack Web Deveoper Program.After completing the course, I am excited to begin my carerrer while growing as a developer.

  • March 2018

    Excited for the future of technology and even more excited to be apart of shaping that future.
