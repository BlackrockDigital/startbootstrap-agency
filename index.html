How it works

The KitSplit rental process in 6 easy steps.

  • GET STARTED: SIGN UP WITH FACEBOOK OR CREATE AN ACCOUNT

  • Set up payments through our secure payment system managed by Braintree (owned by Paypal)

  • Create new listings. You determine the price and who can rent

  • Renters will contact you...and then watch those dollar bill$ roll in

  • Find items you need and request gear

  • Coordinate with the owner/renter, and...
