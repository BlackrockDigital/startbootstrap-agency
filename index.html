About me

A leader by nature and a zealous student of life; always hungry to learn and never afraid of failure.

Having done my first year of EIT Digital Master School at KTH in Stockholm, I am continuing my second year at Aalto University in Helsinki, majoring in Digital Media Technology and minoring in Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

Currently I am building a social startup that empowers refugees arriving to a new land to share their stories while enabling the locals to connect with them, thereby achieving better integration.
